Card Values in Blackjack

Each card in Blackjack has a point value that adds to your total score. Here's how it works:

  • Number Cards (2–10): Worth their face value.
  • Face Cards (J, Q, K): Each is worth 10 points.
  • Ace (A): Can be worth 1 or 11 points – depending on what helps your hand more.

Notes

  • You’ll see these same cards when playing the game.
  • Knowing the values helps you decide when to Hit or Stand.
