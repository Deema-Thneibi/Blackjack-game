How to Play Blackjack

Try to beat the dealer by getting a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Gameplay Steps

  1. You and the dealer each receive two cards. One of the dealer's cards is face up.
  2. You decide whether to Hit (draw a card) or Stand (keep your hand).
  3. If your total goes over 21, you bust and lose the round.
  4. When you stand, the dealer reveals their hidden card and draws more cards until their total is 17 or higher.
  5. The winner is the one with the hand closer to 21 without going over.

Special Scenarios

  • Blackjack: If you get 21 from your first two cards (Ace + 10/J/Q/K), you win immediately — unless the dealer also has Blackjack, then it's a tie.
  • Dealer's Blackjack: If the dealer has Blackjack and you don't, you lose instantly.
  • Bust: If your hand value exceeds 21, you lose automatically.
  • Dealer Bust: If the dealer exceeds 21, you win automatically.
  • Push (Tie): If you and the dealer have the same total (21 or less), it's a tie and no one wins or loses.
  • Higher Hand Wins: If neither busts or has Blackjack, the hand closer to 21 wins.

Tips

  • Always check the dealer's visible card before making a move.
  • If your hand is strong (like 18 or above), consider standing.
  • Remember, Ace is flexible — use it wisely!
